Welcome to Raj Hotel, a luxurious 5-star retreat where sophistication meets comfort. Nestled in the heart of the city, our hotel offers a seamless blend of modern elegance and traditional charm. Indulge in opulent accommodations, exquisite dining experiences, and world-class amenities that ensure an unforgettable stay. At Raj Hotel, every detail is meticulously crafted to provide an unparalleled experience for our guests.

Discover a culinary journey at Raj Hotel with our diverse range of dining options. From the rich flavors of local cuisine to international delicacies, our restaurants cater to every palate. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our state-of-the-art facilities, including conference rooms and a rejuvenating spa, make Raj Hotel the perfect choice for an indulgent and relaxing stay.
